Frequently Asked Questions üí¨

What size pets can travel in each class? +

All classes accommodate pets up to 150 lbs. Economy is perfect for small to medium pets (up to 50 lbs), Premium handles medium to large pets comfortably (up to 100 lbs), and First Class suites can luxuriously accommodate pets of any size with custom arrangements for extra-large breeds.

Can I upgrade my pet's class after booking? +

Absolutely! You can upgrade your pet's travel class up to 24 hours before departure, subject to availability. Just log into your account or call our 24/7 support team. Upgrade fees apply based on the difference in class pricing.

Are multiple pets allowed to share a cabin? +

Yes! Pets from the same family can share cabins in Premium and First Class at no extra charge. Economy class pets travel individually for safety, but we'll ensure family pets are placed near each other.

What's included in the meal service? +

Economy includes standard pet food and water. Premium offers a selection of premium brands and special treats. First Class features custom meal plans, including fresh-prepared options, special dietary accommodations, and gourmet treats. All classes can accommodate prescription diets.
